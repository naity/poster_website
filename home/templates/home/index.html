Innovative research in life science and technology

To foster a unique opportunity for graduate students, postdoctoral fellows, and junior professionals in life science and technology field, the 2nd SABPA Life Science and Technology Poster Event will be held together with the 2nd SABPA Frontiers in Therapeutics and Diagnostics (FTD) Forum. Please attend this exciting event with your poster, showcase your discovery, exchange innovative ideas, and network with peers and leaders from industry and academia, including investment sector.
